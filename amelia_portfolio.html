About Me

Who I Am

Hi! I'm Amelia Nur'aini, a Geographic Information Systems student at Universitas Gadjah Mada with a strong interest in spatial analysis, mapping, and geospatial technology.

I enjoy exploring how spatial data can be transformed into meaningful visual information to support analysis, planning, and decision-making for various environmental and regional issues.

Throughout my academic journey, I have worked on various GIS-related projects involving cartography, remote sensing, spatial data processing, Web GIS development, and environmental analysis using ArcGIS Pro, QGIS, Google Earth Engine, and Python.

I am passionate about combining technical geospatial skills with creativity and problem-solving while continuously learning and growing through both academic and practical experiences.

Web GIS

Wonosobo Wonderlands

Wonosobo Wonderlands is an interactive Web GIS platform that visualizes tourist destinations across Wonosobo Regency. It features interactive mapping, geolocation, random destination suggestions, and full CRUD functionality for managing tourism data. Built with Laravel, Leaflet.js, PostGIS, and Bootstrap, the platform also supports basemap switching and real-time spatial digitization.
